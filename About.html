PulseCare is web-based fundraising software to empower non-profits, individuals and the causes they support.

About Us

PulseCare is a purpose-oriented business, owned by its employees, dedicated to assisting the nonprofit sector with online fundraising and donor engagement since 2006. Our commitment has extended to supporting over 150,000 causes and serving as the technology partner for numerous community-led Giving Days.

We operate as a compact yet highly capable team, experiencing firsthand the challenges of working for a nonprofit organization with limited resources in pursuit of your mission. Our aim is to assist you with a potent online fundraising suite, exceptional customer support, and strategic fundraising resources, all geared towards ensuring your success.

We provide a portfolio of state-of-the-art online and mobile products including; Online Fundraising, Donor Management, Team and Event Fundraising, Custom Donation Pages and Widgets, Giving Day Platform and Personal Crowdfunding. We are at the forefront of function, experience, and style, and are committed to building you the best and boldest technologies in online fundraising.
