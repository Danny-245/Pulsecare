WHAT YOUR GIFT MEANS:

As a small non-profit, our work is directly tied to donations. If we do not raise enough money, we cannot provide healthcare or invest in long term solutions to resource-poor communities. Our staff is unpaid, and our overhead expenses are limited to website subscriptions, organizational expenses (legal, etc.), and general supplies to keep us up and running.

In 2019, we spent 96% of all donations on providing care to rural areas. This includes mobilizing our care team, purchasing critical medicines, and maintaining strong ties to our partner, FIBUSPAM.

Your gift goes directly to buying medicines for those in need and will impact the lives of so many. A small gift of $100 can provide 40 cervical cancer screening tests for women, or provide full medical treatment for 5 patients. Any bit helps those that need care and creates a positive impact for their lives.
